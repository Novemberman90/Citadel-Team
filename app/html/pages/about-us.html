About Us

Our Mission: Your Digital Freedom Fortress

In an era where digital surveillance and censorship are on the rise, we believe that everyone has a right to a free and open internet. Our mission is to provide you with a powerful, secure, and easy-to-use tool to protect your online privacy and bypass restrictions. Citadel VPN is your fortress in the digital world.

Who We Are

We are a team of privacy advocates, cybersecurity experts, and technology enthusiasts dedicated to building a better internet. We were founded on the principles of privacy, security, and freedom. We believe that your data is your own, and we are committed to protecting it.

What We Do

Citadel VPN provides a state-of-the-art VPN service built on the modern and efficient VLESS protocol. Our service is designed to be fast, reliable, and secure, allowing you to browse the web without borders. We are a Telegram-first service, offering a seamless and intuitive user experience through our powerful Telegram bot. This allows for quick and easy setup, management of your subscription, and access to our global network of servers.

Our Values

  • Privacy: Your privacy is our top priority. We have a strict no-logs policy, meaning we never track, store, or share your online activity.
  • Security: We use military-grade encryption and the latest VPN protocols to keep your data safe from prying eyes.
  • Freedom: We believe in an open internet for everyone. Our service helps you bypass censorship and access the content you want, wherever you are.
  • Transparency: We are committed to being open and honest with our users. We believe that trust is earned through transparency.

Why Choose Citadel VPN?

  • Modern Technology: We use the VLESS protocol, which is designed for performance and security.
  • Telegram-First: Manage your VPN directly from Telegram, one of the world's most popular and secure messaging apps.
  • Global Network: Access our network of high-speed servers in locations around the world.
  • No-Logs Policy: We never log your activity. Your privacy is guaranteed.
  • 24/7 Support: Our dedicated support team is always here to help you.
